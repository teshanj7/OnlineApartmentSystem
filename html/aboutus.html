About Us

Pegasus Apartments is a business organization that works hard for the fulfillment of their customers' apartment needs . It is a system where sellers could come and post advertisements for their apartments and lets customers book apartment inspections and buy their dream home afterwards. Our company was started on the 6th of June 2010. Our business organization started off at a small rented house, now in the present we have our own building situated at 123, Green Path, Colombo 07. Pegasus Customer has partnered with popular island wide banks and insurance companies such as Sampath Bank, Commercial Bank, AIA Insurance to ensure the purchasing procedure is safe and efficient. Our main projects are funded mainly by John Keels PLC and Softlogic Holdings. With many projects and apartments getting posted by the reputed sellers around the island the pegasus team works hard to bring a fresh new experience to their customers who visit them in search of their dream apartment. We at Pegasus Apartments strive everyday to bring you closer to your dream home.

Our Vision

Together we are creating an apartment sales system, one with a leading role in assiting our customers' dreams of their new home. By focusing on you as a customer or a seller, we strive daily for the satisfaction of our system's users. We are together building a system to create a community that will help our users' needs.
